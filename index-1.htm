The PHAINO company was founded in 2009 as a "PHENOTYPE" project, an office of architecture and design that for more than 3 years was dedicated exclusively to the study, selection of materials and development of manufacturing processes that defined our first collections of kitchen.

Simultaneously, ties and relationships with specialist companies were created with the infrastructure and experience necessary to provide the highest quality of workmanship, complemented by the PHENOTYPE design team, resulting in a workshop specialized in high-end kitchens with an emphasis on functional and sophisticated design.

Our name "PHAINO" comes from the Latin meaning "to shine", a concept that represents the pride of design and manufacture of Mexican labor, so we are always in search of maximum simplicity and beauty.

In PHAINO we believe that the kitchen is the most important space of the house, being the place where we spend most of the time, whether with family, friends or even in business meetings.

This conviction leads us to develop customized kitchen furniture projects, based on the individual needs of each user, the ecological importance in the selection of materials and the ergonomic approach to the health, posture and comfort of the human being.

Based on the concept of essentials, we identified our six cooking collections through basic geometric signs we learned from children, before we could understand any word.

Collections

Circle

Circle

Traditional Contemporary, an oxymoron that defines the line CIRCLE, basic collection created with simple lines, with a subtle countertop of 5 centimeters thick embraces the low cabinets reaching the floor and giving a great hierarchy to the work area.

X

X

Of combined concepts, "the work table"; The beginning of residential kitchen furniture, with a strong thick countertop that reaches 9 centimeters and seeks to maintain the areas of preparation, cooking and washing functional line.

The second concept, inspired by the "NAKED" motorcycling culture, shows the soul of the furniture, showing the pipes and installations perfectly and cleanly. With "X" shaped legs the result is a bold line of imposing character.

Triangle

Triangle

Solid and with well-defined orthogonal volumes, its solides show monumentality. Its striking countertop of only 12 mm thick fuses with doors and drawer fronts resulting in a very sober design.

Square

Square

Robust and extremely dynamic collection, its 7 centimeters thick countertop can be extended up to 1.8 meters without bases or additional supports. Channels run horizontally all along the fronts of drawers and doors creating a set of lines and an impression of areas that are wider than they really are.

Pentagon

Pentagon

The lightest of our collections, designed with a compact floating cabinet that is completely frame by a 12 millimeter thick countertop, gives the impression that furniture floats in the kitchen space, when they have on the walls embed to these, and in islands they are supported on bases in form of inverted "V". PENTAGON allows us to appreciate the total extension of the walls and floor, benefiting small kitchen spaces making the kitchen area seem larger.

Hexagon

Hexagon

Inverted concept, with hierarchy on the furniture fronts, its countertop seems invisible until the moment the doors are opened. The MURAL SYSTEM can be applied in the perimeter channel between the front and the countertop, adding functions to the work area.

The countertop is designed with a border around its perimeter that prevents liquids from spilling, the attached bars extend to the ground with complex and marked angles completing the final design of the islands in finishes such as wood, stainless steel or solid surface.

Design Profile

Quality

The use of innovative and certified materials (glass, aluminum, stainless steel, ceramics, etc.) based on a main quality, which are high strength.

The exclusive system of assemblies and our lightened aluminum door, as well as the care in the finishes in veneer of wood, lacquers and varnishes allow us to deliver a product of the highest quality.

In the same way the fittings that are integrated in all the PHAINO furniture allow us to handle the highest guarantees of the market.

Ecology

We have designed clean manufacturing processes with Mexican craftsmanship combined with new technologies.

We improve the manufacturing processes by designing them as assemblies instead of bonding with adhesives, in this way we reduce the use of adhesives by reducing formaldehyde considerably.

The development of aluminum frames on all the doors and drawer fronts allow in the future an easy disassembly allowing to recycle and reuse most of the materials of our kitchens.

Ergonomics

From the selection of materials to the qualities of the end user, taking their particular physical aspects (height, whether right or left, etc), we live the design service with great importance, always proposing projects according to the particular way of living the kitchen for each user, under their specific needs and their aesthetic tastes.

Thinking about the future, we always transmit to our customers new and better uses that will make your life more practical, for less than 20 years after the acquisition of your PHAINO kitchen.

Systems

All

All

Complementary to all collections of PHAINO kitchens, of essential aesthetics is modular system of high cabinets and column cabinets. It allows the organization of storage areas and pantry to remain always within reach in the same kitchen.

ALL System matches some of the most important accessories of the kitchen, such as refrigeration zones, freezers and wine cellars, cookers such as ovens, microwaves and also dishwashers, built-in, integrable and panelables are completely merged with the cabinet passing unnoticed, giving a clean and attractive image.

Sense

Sense

The most importan system of all our kitchens, conformed by the body of the cabinets, the working countertops and the fronts. None of these can be lacking for the existence of the kitchen.

It is the main point where we achieve the desired aesthetics for each project, selecting together with the user the materials, colors and finishes of your kitchen.

Dynamic

Dynamic

In order to make a kitchen work we must keep it in order, DYNAMIC system allows the organization inside each drawer with integrated accessories for all possible uses and completely adaptable.

The magnetic organizers attach to the bottom of the drawer and are adapted to all types of utensils and small appliances according to the needs of the user.

Cutlery holders, small electrical appliances, cutlery, baking pans, open vegetable drawers, dish organizers and saucepans are just some of the organizer accessories.

Art

Art

Personalization is extremely important to us, ART system is extremely fun and invites you to live the kitchen with creativity. PHAINO kitchens are offered with a simple design engraved in all their drawers and some additional options to customer selection.

It is possible a unique special design defined with the advice of our design team, engraved in each drawer with the highest technology allows each user to extend their personality to the kitchen space, a detail that only your kitchen and no other can match.

Mural

Mural

We all need an assistant when we cook, MURAL system is our personal Souschef that keeps all the common accessories and utensils always within reach, facilitating the tasks in the kitchen, improving working time and avoiding accidents.
